Organisation Glastonbury Child and Family Services (1984 - )

From
1984
Geelong
Categories
Care Provider, Community Service Organisation, Non-denominational and Records Service
Website
http://www.glastonbury.org.au/

Summary

Glastonbury Child and Family Services came into being in 1984. Previously, it was known as Glastonbury Children's Home. The history of the agency dates back to 1854 and the establishment of the Geelong Orphan Asylum. The name change reflected the agency's new directions and service philosophies.
