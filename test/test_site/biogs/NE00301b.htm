Hartwell House (1960 - 1970)

  • Summary
  • Full page

  • Nardy Old Boys & Girls Reunion Club, 2005, courtesy of The Remmers.
    Details

From
1960
To
1970
Categories
Home and Non-denominational

Hartwell House was a Dr Barnardo's Home that opened in 1960 at Kiama. It closed in 1970.

Details

In 1994 a former housemaster of Hartwell House, Vic Holyoak, was sentenced to 10 years jail for sexually abusing children placed in his care.
